Account Deletion Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

You can request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time. Deletion is permanent and irreversible.

1. How to delete your account

Option 1 — In the App (recommended)

Go to Settings → Delete Account in the SLP Command app and follow the confirmation steps. Your account is scheduled for permanent deletion immediately upon confirmation.

Option 2 — By email

Send a request from your account email address to privacy@slpcommand.com with the subject line "Delete my account", including your account email in the message body for identity verification. We will process verified requests within 30 days.

2. What is permanently removed

Upon account deletion, the following is permanently and irreversibly deleted within 30 days:

  • Your account and login credentials.
  • All Speaking audio recordings and their storage-layer copies.
  • All written responses and AI-generated transcripts.
  • All scores, estimated levels, and exercise history.
  • All Adaptive Coach and Intelligence Dashboard data derived from your results.
  • All support requests submitted through "Report a Problem" that have already been resolved at the time of deletion.

3. What may be retained, and why

Security audit trail. A pseudonymous record of security-relevant account events (login, account creation, account deletion) is retained for 12 months from the date of the event, as described in full in the Data Retention Policy. This record does not contain your email, content, or any directly identifying information, and does not prevent your erasure request from being honoured for every other category of data.

Legal obligations. We may retain the minimum data strictly required to comply with a legal obligation (for example, fraud-prevention or accounting records, where applicable), for no longer than legally required, and never for any other purpose.

4. Third-party processors

Sub-processors that received your Content during your use of the Service (see the Subprocessors page) are contractually bound to delete or anonymise it in accordance with their own data processing agreements with us. Some processors (notably AI providers) may retain transient API request logs for a short period under their own security/abuse-prevention policies, independent of our systems; this is disclosed in the Privacy Policy and does not extend the retention of any data within our own infrastructure.

5. Your rights

Account deletion exercises your right to erasure under GDPR Art. 17. For other data rights (access, portability, rectification), contact privacy@slpcommand.com or see the Privacy Policy.
