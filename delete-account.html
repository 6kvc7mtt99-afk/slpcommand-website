Delete Your Account

Last updated: 29 June 2026

You can request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time. Deletion is permanent and irreversible.

Option 1 — In the App (recommended)

Go to Settings → Delete Account in the SLP Command app. Follow the confirmation steps. Your account and all data will be scheduled for permanent deletion.

Option 2 — By email

Send a request from your account email address to privacy@slpcommand.com with the subject line "Delete my account". Include your account email in the message body so we can verify your identity.

We will process verified deletion requests within 30 days.

What is permanently removed

  • Your account and login credentials.
  • All audio recordings (Speaking exercises).
  • All written responses and transcripts.
  • All scores, estimated levels, and exercise history.
  • All Adaptive Coach and Intelligence data derived from your results.

What may be retained

We may retain certain data for a limited period where required by law (e.g. accounting records, fraud prevention logs). Such data is kept only as long as legally required and is not used for any other purpose.

Your rights

Account deletion exercises your right to erasure under GDPR Art. 17. For other data rights (access, portability, rectification), contact privacy@slpcommand.com or see the Privacy Policy.
