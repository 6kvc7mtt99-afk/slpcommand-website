Statement of principles

Data minimisation

Registration requires only an email address and a password. We do not ask for your name, rank, unit, service branch, or location, ever.

No advertising

SLP Command does not use advertising identifiers, does not sell personal data, and does not track you across other apps or websites.

Consent-based AI audio processing

Sending your voice to an AI provider for evaluation is always your explicit, separate, revocable choice — never a condition of using the rest of the Service.

Transparent sub-processors

Every third party that touches your data is listed, with its purpose, the data it receives, and its location, on our Subprocessors page — kept current with every infrastructure change.

Independent and honest about limitations

SLP Command is not affiliated with NATO, any Ministry of Defence, or any official examining body, and never represents its AI Feedback as an Official Assessment.
