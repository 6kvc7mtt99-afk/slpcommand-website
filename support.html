Support

Last updated: 29 June 2026

Need help, found a bug, or have a question about your data? We're here for you.

Contact us

  • General support & bug reports: support@slpcommand.com
  • Privacy & data requests (GDPR / erasure): privacy@slpcommand.com
  • Response time: we aim to reply within 48 hours on business days.

Languages / Idiomas

We handle support requests in English and Spanish (Español).

Atendemos solicitudes de soporte en inglés y español. Puedes escribirnos en el idioma que prefieras.

What to include in your message

  • The email address associated with your account.
  • A description of the issue or question.
  • The iOS version and device model (if reporting a bug).
  • Any screenshots that help illustrate the problem.

Do not send passwords or payment details.

Account deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, go to Settings → Delete Account in the App, or see Delete Account for full instructions.
