Practice Mode

Exercises adapt in real time to your estimated level and to weak sub-skills specifically — not just overall difficulty. A spaced-review scheduler resurfaces items you got wrong until they stick.

📋

Exam Simulation

Timed, full-length mock exams for Reading, Listening, Speaking and Writing, built to the SLP 2 and SLP 3 formats separately — question count, time limits and scoring bands match the level you select.

📚

Academy

11 structured topics per skill — grammar and strategy references, dozens of worked examples with step-by-step reasoning, and a short quiz per topic with immediate, explained feedback.

🧠

Intelligence Dashboard

Readiness score, a per-skill weakness profile, and a "brain profile" of strengths, weak spots and recent growth — recomputed after every single session, not on a fixed schedule.

🤖

AI Coaching

Speaking is transcribed with OpenAI Whisper and scored across five dimensions — fluency, grammar, vocabulary, coherence and task achievement. Writing is scored on task achievement, content & organisation, and language precision, with an improved-version rewrite for comparison.

📈

Mastery & Progression

Long-term accuracy trends (7/30/90-day windows) per skill, with a mastered / developing / needs-work status and an estimated attempts-to-mastery figure — so progress is measurable, not just a single score.

🧭

Adaptive Coach

A short, prioritised list of what to practise next, generated from your actual weakness profile — not generic tips. Updates as your profile changes.

🔁

Cross-device sync

Your account, progress, scores and Academy status sync automatically — pick up training on any device without losing history.
